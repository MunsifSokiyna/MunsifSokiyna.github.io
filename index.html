I am a Ph.D. student in Management Information Systems at The University of Alabama (UA) under the supervision of Dr. Pratyush Nidhi Sharma . I also obtained an MSc in Business Informatics (E-Business) from Jadara University in 2016. My expertise and research interests lie at the intersection of Information Systems, Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and the dynamics of Open Source Software (OSS). My research is dedicated to understanding and mitigating Online Toxicity within digital platforms..
